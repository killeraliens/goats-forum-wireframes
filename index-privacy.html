Privacy

Your personal account information will never be shared, but the things you post on here may be.

Some of the data obtained by this website will eventually be made accessablie to other developers as an API resource. This means something you post (like a venue or flyer) may be used for future alternative apps and front-facing websites (with our approval).

We are working on getting forum account holders more control on what other members may see, but for now, everything you post here will be public to other members.

We want better tools for you, the bands, fans, and people putting on the shows. Public event data is currently owned by monopolies like Facebook and has never been more difficult to gain access to from a developers standpoint. This forum intends to build an independant data resource for the events we want more of.
